Become A Counselor


Your first step at becoming a part of the "Royaneh Familia!"


As a counselor at camp you must look after your cabin group and day group campers and help them through the week with meals, hikes, bathing, and, most importantly, having the greatest experience possible.

Detailed information


Interview

To be a camp counselor, you must interview. Our interview process is very easy and engaging. We ask a combination of silly and serious questions in a group setting to determine if you have what it takes!

Pre Camp Training

A week before we head up to the magical grounds of Camp Royaneh, counselors old and new come together to play ice breaker games, make camp signage, and discuss important camp related topics.

Week of Camp Expectations

As a counselor, it is your responsibility to tend to and look after the well-being of our campers and fellow staff. You must be enthusiastic and willing to make lasting memories for everyone there.

All first year counselors will ride the counselor bus to camp and arrive to camp on Friday April 14th the day before the campers. We will all work together to setup by prepping cabins, tent sites, and other facilities for the week and also go over important information and, most importantly, create that initial bond
