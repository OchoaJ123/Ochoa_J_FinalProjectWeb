About Event


Life Skills After Covid


The Old Adobe Union School District hosts 6th grade camp at a Boy Scout Camp, called Camp Royaneh. The events will include hikes, camp team-building exercises, and survival life skill team-buildingexercises.


Event Day 1: In Camp


"In Camp Day" is a dynamic experience tailored for 6th graders to rebuild teamwork post-COVID. Through team-building games, trust falls, and a ropes course, students rediscover collaboration and unity.


This day of fun activities serves as a refreshing opportunity for 6th graders to strengthen bonds and rekindle cooperative skills after the challenges of the pandemic.

Event Day 2: River Hike


"River Hike Day" is an exciting adventure where counselors lead sixth graders on a scenic hike to a river. Amidst nature, students engage in activities like archery, bracelet-making, and a unique "blind walk."


During the blind walk, students, guided by adult counselors, navigate a short hike blindfolded, relying solely on the sounds of the forest. This immersive experience not only fosters teamwork but also enhances sensory awareness, creating lasting memories for the sixth graders

Event Day 3: Survival Day


"Survival Day" empowers sixth graders as they build tents from wood and sticks, enhancing teamwork. Through group activities, students form team names and chants, fostering unity.


Learning survival skills, such as building fires, becomes a metaphor for overcoming obstacles. Fun water activities add joy, creating a positive and memorable experience for students navigating the post-pandemic landscape together.

Event Day 4: Ocean Hike


"Ocean Hike Day" takes sixth graders on a learning adventure about ocean animals. Students explore and discover marine life in an engaging environment. Despite the challenges of COVID, this experience offers a safe and rejuvenating outdoor setting, providing a welcome break from virtual learning and promoting mental well-being.


Connecting with nature and marine life fosters a sense of wonder, helping students navigate the difficulties of the pandemic by creating positive and memorable experiences during these challenging times.

End Of Day Activities


At the end of the day, the excitement continues with "Camp Show" and "Camp Dance." Sixth graders let loose and have a blast as they showcase their talents during the camp show. The camp dance follows, offering a lively space for students to unwind, socialize, and create lasting memories.


These activities not only provide a fun conclusion to the day but also serve as a joyful outlet, fostering positive connections among the sixth graders and helping them unwind from the challenges of the day and the broader context of the COVID era.
